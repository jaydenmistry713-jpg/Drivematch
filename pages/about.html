Hi, I'm Jayden

I built DriveMatch because I got frustrated watching people make expensive mistakes buying cars they didn't really need. The car-buying process is broken — it starts with inventory instead of the person.

DriveMatch flips it around. We start with you — your life, your budget, your needs — and work backwards to the right car. Then I personally source it, arrange an independent check, and handle the process.

I have no commercial relationship with any manufacturer or dealer. My only incentive is to find you the right car.
