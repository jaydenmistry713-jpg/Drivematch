How It Works

From profile to perfect car — here's exactly what happens, and why it works better than anything else.

01
📋

Complete Your Profile

Answer 8–10 questions about your budget, how you drive, your household size, fuel preference, annual mileage, and what matters most. The more detail you give, the more accurate the match.

We ask about things most car sites ignore — your parking situation, whether you tow anything, how long you sit in traffic. These details matter more than you'd think.
02
🤖

AI Analyses Your Needs

Claude AI by Anthropic maps your answers against hundreds of vehicle attributes — real-world running costs, reliability data, boot space, fuel efficiency, insurance group, and suitability for your specific situation.

This is not keyword matching or simple filtering. The AI understands context — it knows that "large family + long motorway" and "large family + city only" lead to very different optimal vehicles.
03
📊

Receive Your Match Report

Your primary recommendation comes with a compatibility score, 5 honest strengths, considerations to be aware of, a personalised summary that references your specific answers, and 3 alternative vehicles.

We tell you the downsides too. If a car has a notoriously complex infotainment system or expensive servicing, you need to know before you commit.
04
🔍

We Find It For You

If you want the car, click "Find this for me." We personally source it, verify its history, arrange an independent vehicle inspection, and connect you directly with the seller.

No showroom visits unless you want one. No pushy sales. Just a car that matches your profile, checked and ready.

Common Questions

Is DriveMatch free to use?
Completely free for buyers. DriveMatch makes money from a small commission when a deal closes with one of our seller partners — the buyer never pays.
How accurate is the AI matching?
The AI is excellent at filtering hundreds of options down to the most genuinely suitable vehicles. No algorithm is perfect — it is a recommendation, not a mandate. The pros and cons in your report give you everything you need to make an informed decision.
Do I have to buy the matched vehicle?
No. Your match is a recommendation. You can use it as a starting point, read reviews, explore the alternatives, or come back and run a new quiz with different answers.
What happens when I click "Find this for me"?
Jayden personally reviews your enquiry and reaches out within 24 hours. From there, he sources options that match your profile and budget, arranges vehicle checks, and walks you through the process.
Does DriveMatch arrange finance?
Not at this stage. DriveMatch is purely a vehicle matching and sourcing service. We connect you with sellers. Finance arrangements are between you and your preferred lender.
What if I disagree with my match?
Run the quiz again with different answers, or click "Start over." You can also use the "Also consider" alternatives on your result page. Every profile is different and small changes in your answers can produce meaningfully different results.
